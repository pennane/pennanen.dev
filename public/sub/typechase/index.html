Typechase information

What exactly?

Typechase -the writing game, of sorts. Meant for personal typing performance practice and inspection.

How to?

Open the main window and simply start typing the content of the window.

After finishing typing you will be presented your stats; speed in WPM (words per minute) and accuracy as a percentage calculated from missed characters.

Typechase stores your attempts in local storage allowing to come back and try to see possible stat improvements.
